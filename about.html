FARMER KING
Farmer King is the web application which creates a direct interface between the farmers and retailers.
In this the retailer will be provided an interface so that he can specify which goods he want to buy and
its quantity. He will also specifies his price for the goods. In this manner every retailer species his own
requirements. Now farmer specifies the quantity and the type of goods he has. Now the farmer will be
provided a list of retailers who wants to buy the goods he has, so he can sell his goods for the maximum profit.
